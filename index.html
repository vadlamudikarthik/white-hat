Viswabharati High School is a world-class day-cum-boarding school located in the picturesque small-town of Gudivada, Andhra Pradesh. Established 5 decades ago, it is now widely recognised as one of the best schools not just in Andhra Pradesh but in all of PAN India. Combining traditional values and teaching methods with a modern learning and living infrastructure, the school today is home to over 2000 day-scholars and 2000 thousand borders. Over the last 52 years, Viswabharati has been the leading centre for emerging trends in education. From transitioning to a full English medium curriculum 37 years ago to pioneering Foundation test-prep courses for IIT and medicine a decade ago, the school has many firsts to its name. It was one the first schools in India to have dedicated computer lab. The teaching methodology and training standards in Viswabharati are considered benchmark setting and are now being followed in a number of places in Andhra Pradesh. The school is also known for inculcating a strong sense of traditional values and moral sense of purpose in its students.
